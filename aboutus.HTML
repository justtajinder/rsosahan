Our Product Range

Heavy Rolling Turning Machine

Built with extra heavy-duty one-piece construction, our machines feature high damping coefficients and minimal thermal deformation. The 4-bed design ensures optimal cutting for heavy-duty applications.

Facing Lathe Machine

Perfect for creating flat surfaces perpendicular to the axis of rotation. Our facing lathes offer high precision and smooth operations, enhancing machining performance.

All Gear Head / Semi Gear Head Lathe

These versatile machines are ideal for turning, facing, drilling, threading, and tapering. Our semi gear head lathes combine the benefits of both gear head and cone pulley systems for enhanced flexibility and control.
